The regression line tries to map the linear association between the variable $X \& Y$
Where,
	$X=[x_1, x_2, \dots, x_n]$
	$Y=[y_1, y_2, \dots, y_n]$
To find this line of regression, recall the equation of a line
	$\hat{y_i}=a+bx_{i}$
	Where, *a* is the intercept and *b* is the slope
